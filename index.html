At Femme in Tech, we know every woman has the power to be a leader in tech YOU have the skills, talent, and creativity needed to thrive in this dynamic and exciting field. Now let's share these with others and help them succeed.

That's why we are on a mission to empower women in tech with mentorship, resources and a community where educating others about the successes of women in tech today and throughout history is at our heart. Let's celebrate and share these stories to inspire and uplift the next generation.

Join us to start debugging the challenges women face today!

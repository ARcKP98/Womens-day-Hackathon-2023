Donate and Support


Every contribution helps. Even if you don't think so.


Donations might not seem like a big task but, most organisations that help women progress in the industry are mostly non-profit organisations. Donations is one of their key sources of fuding without which it can be difficult for these organisations to operate. If you feel like donating to further this cause, you can find two excellent organisations that could use your help.


WITU


The Women In Technology Uganda(WITU) is a non-profit that focuses on empowering and helping girls and women from underserved communities by offering them education in STEM related fields by providing better tools, resources and opportunities that allow them to thrive in the industry and as a result, in the world. The WITU also helps with personal development, mentoring, life skills, health, business skills, job linkages, and smart loans to further encourage women to explore their full potential.

AnitaB.Org


Anita Borg was a computer scientist in the 1980's who founded a community for women in computing. Since its inception, it has grown and become one of the leading organisations for women in tech. The organisation has a global reach and works with Fortune 500 companies and academic institutions. This organisation not only works towards increasing the influence of women in tech industry but also promotes creation of technology that will have a positive imapct on women all around the world.
